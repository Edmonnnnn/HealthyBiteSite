Contact FAQ

Quick answers to the most common things people ask before reaching out.

How fast do you usually reply?

Most messages receive an answer within 24 hours on business days.

Can I get medical advice here?

No. We do not diagnose or replace medical care. We can help you prepare questions for your doctor or think through habits.

Do you offer meal plans for families?

Yes, we can discuss flexible structures that work for shared meals and different appetites.

Can I cancel or reschedule a consultation?

Yes, just reply to our email or use the link inside the confirmation message.

Do you store my messages forever?

We keep only what we need to support you and improve the service, and you can ask us to delete your data.
